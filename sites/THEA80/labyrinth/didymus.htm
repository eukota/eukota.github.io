Sir Didymus

Sir Didymus and his faithful steed (dog) Ambrosious are the bravest characters in the film, constantly pestering the goblin guards, or whatever other character happens to get in their way, including Ludo at one point. They were sworn to protect the bridge leading out of the Bog of Eternal Stench, and had never let anyone across until Sarah asked permission to do so. The voice of Sir Didymus is DAVID SHAUGHNESSY and he is controlled by DAVE GOELZ and DAVID BARCLAY.
