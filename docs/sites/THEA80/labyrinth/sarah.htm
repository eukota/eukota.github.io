Sarah

Sarah, the main protagonist in the film is a teenage girl who does not want to grow up, but finds that she has to when one of her games sends her baby brother, Toby away with the Goblin King. Sarah loves her toys and stuffed animals, as well as plying dress up with her favorite book, Labyrinth. By the end of the film, Sarah has matured considerably, and she returns from the Labyrinth with a new sense of priorites, as well as a great group of new friends. Sarah is played by actress Jennifer Connelly.
