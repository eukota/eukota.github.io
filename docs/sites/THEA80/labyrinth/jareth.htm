Jareth

Jareth the Goblin King, played by DAVID BOWIE, is the second main character in the movie. He wants to keep Toby, Sarah's baby brother so that he can turn him into a goblin, and continually trys to throw Sarah off the track in her quest to get Toby back. He is very powerful and can manipulate most of the creatures of the Labyrinth, except for Sarah, whose will is stronger than his. His attempts to make Sarah go home fail, and in the end, he is unsuccessful in keeping Toby as his own.
