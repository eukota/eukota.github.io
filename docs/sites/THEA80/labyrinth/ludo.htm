Ludo

Ludo is the gigantic, "Where The Wild Things Are" monster creature, who is by far the most lovable creature in the movie. Ludo is very shy as well as being a bit cowardly. He becomes Sarah's friend after she rescues him from the "nippy nips" that the goblin guards have been torturing him with.
